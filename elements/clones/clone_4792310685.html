FAQ Section
What is the FAQ section?
The FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions) section is a place where you can provide answers to common questions that users may have about your product, service, or website.
How do I use the accordion?
The accordion allows users to toggle the visibility of the answer by clicking on the question. Only one answer is visible at a time, and clicking on another question will close the previously opened answer.
Can I add more questions?
Yes, you can add as many questions and answers as you want. Just duplicate the "faq-item" div and replace the question and answer text.
